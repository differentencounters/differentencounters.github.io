Our Team

Ray N. Fredrick III

Ray N. Fredrick III is a graduate of the United States Military Academy where he earned three letters as a member of the varsity men’s basketball team and served as a team captain during his senior year. After a successful five-year active duty military service to his country, Ray completed his master’s degree in physical education from Teachers College, Columbia University.

While at Columbia, Ray successfully established several afterschool physical activity programs including two highly successful jump rope programs at the The Nightingale-Bamford School and 92nd Street Y in Manhattan. In addition to after-school programming, Ray has served as a teacher in several New York City public schools with his latest position being as a physical education instructor at Teachers College Community School during the 2011-2012 school year. Currently, Ray is a physical education teacher in the Eastchester School District located in Westchester County, New York.

Andrey Yoffe

Andrey A. Yoffe currently serves in an investment banking role at Barclays Capital, focused on the Aerospace and Defense industry. Andrey has been at Barclays since 2014 and has over 7 years of investment banking experience.

Previously, Andrey founded a local activities start-up company with a focus on stimulating activeness and healthy social interaction in people’s lives.

Andrey completed his bachelor’s degree in finance and international business from the Stern School of Business at New York University.

Risto Marttinen

Risto Marttinen is a graduate of California State University, Fullerton where he received his Bachelors degree in Health Science and his Masters Degree in Kinesiology with an emphasis in Sports Psychology. He is currently attending Teachers College, Columbia University for his Doctorate Degree in Education in Curriculum and working as a physical education teacher at the Teachers College Community School in Harlem teaching Pre-K through 2nd grade physical education.

Risto’s background includes college wrestling for Fullerton’s Division I wrestling program where he was the Team Captain. Subsequently, he became head assistant coach where he was in charge of recruiting.

Risto brings to Directed Energy an understanding of sports psychology, teaching, coaching and an in-depth understanding of the college sports world.

Amanda Borbee

Amanda is a recent graduate of Adelphi University with a B.S in Physical Education and a minor in Sports Management. While attending Adelphi University she was a two year member of the Varsity Field Hockey team.

Currently, Amanda is working with the NYC Wellness in the Schools program, while pursing a Masters in Sports Management, with a specialization in Sport-Based Youth Development at Adelphi University.

Anthony Villanueva

Anthony Villanueva graduated with a B.A. in Ethnic Studies (Option in Asian American Studies) and a B.S. in Kinesiology (Concentration in Strength & Conditioning). Along with graduating cum laude, he is the 2016 Craig and Claire Ihara Outstanding Student Award awardee.

Anthony has been working as a research assistant for California State University, Fullerton in collaboration with the Orange County Asian Pacific Islander Community Alliance at the Healthy Asian Pacific Islander Youth Empowerment Program at Magnolia High School. Additionally, he has been coaching in the Strength and Conditioning program at Servite High School. He will be attending CSUF for a master’s degree in Kinesiology in hopes to help increase the quality of life of community members through exercise.

Mitchell Clayton

Mitchell Clayton is a Vice President and Portfolio Analyst on Lazard Asset Management’s Alternative Emerging Markets investment team. She has worked in the financial services industry for 13 years.

Mitch is a native New Yorker. As a power forward at Hunter College High School, she was named to the New York Newsday All-Manhattan 2nd team. She has served as coach, instructor, and referee in the Safe Haven West Side Basketball League, the neighborhood league in which she played as a girl. In addition, she plays in several regular pick-up basketball games.

Michael Rakosi

Michael Rakosi wears many hats and he wears them well. In addition to being President of The Garden School in Jackson Heights, Queens, and a very successful NYC real estate developer over the years, Mike happens to own one of the largest basketball card collections, he has his own YouTube show and is a fantastic stand-up comedian as well.

What we are most proud of and grateful for, is Michael's role as a member of the Board at Directed Energy and Chair of our Fundraising team.
