DE MISSION:

to positively engage adolescent youth in learning about themselves through physical, character, and health education in a weekly, out-of-school atmosphere.

Directed Energy (“DE”) is a not-for- profit organization dedicated to the development of under-resourced, pre-adolescent youth in New York City and Los Angeles. DE’s goal is to positively engage youth in learning about themselves through education in a weekly, out-of- school atmosphere focusing on physical, character and health education.

In its 3-year history, DE has served 100+ adolescents in 3 partner schools.
